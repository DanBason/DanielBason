Research Question

What makes social media so addicting, and how can technology be used to effectively explain the harmful effects to students?

Synthesis of Research Pages

Social Media has been proven to cause anxiety, stress, and social isolation. Social media companies employ practices such as "endless scrolling" and "nudging," a way to subtly tell people to get on or stay on an app, to addict many young people. Bringing awareness to the issue by explaining the negative effects is a potential solution. Likewise, technology is an importnat tool that can help us limit our time on such social medias by limiting what sites we can even allow (see case study).

I'd say the most important idea that I've learned throughout this research is that social media companies employ nefarious behavioral tactics to profit off the suffering of users. If we are going to create a society that doesn't depend upon the validation of others, young people need to be educated about the practices and effects of social media.

Using the idea of commitment structures (I think is the term), I'm going to create an app that decentivizes students from using social media. This may involve connecting them to another person who has a similar go, or using money.
